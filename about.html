The Story of Southern Flair

Brandi Alexander is from a small town in Breckinridge County, KY, where she is currently raising her two sons, Mikey and Colt. After nearly a decade of teaching elementary school, Brandi auditioned for season 7 of Fox's hit show MasterChef. She wanted the opportunity to show her children and her students that if they dream big and work hard, their dreams really can come true. After making it on the show, Brandi clawed her way through the judging to become one of the top 20 home cooks in America, and ultimately completed the show as a finalist. Upon returning home from the show, Brandi decided to make the leap and open her own full time catering business, Southern Flair with Chef Brandi.
