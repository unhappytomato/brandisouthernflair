Testimonials

Master Chef, Brandi Alexander came to my house and prepared a surprise birthday dinner for my daughter’s 50th birthday. She made everything so easy for me! The only difficult part was deciding which menu options I would choose!

For our appetizer, we had apricot bourbon bbq meatballs. Boy were they a hit! Our salad was an apple cranberry spring mix with her homemade golden balsamic vinaigrette, followed by the main course, pistachio crusted cod with parsnip purée, and roasted garlic mashed potatoes. The best fish I’ve ever put in my mouth! We ended with a delicious apple crisp with her special bourbon caramel sauce. The food was outstanding!

Brandi is a very charming, personable young lady, who presented each dish with a smile and a wonderful description of each plate. I could not have been more pleased with the meal or the evening, and to top it all off, she cleaned up the kitchen! I felt like it was my birthday.

We loved our food and service when we had a family picnic catered. Everyone loved the apricot Bourbon shredded pork BBQ, the pineapple chicken skewers, and sausage cheddar balls. The highlight of the savory dishes was definitely the apple cranberry slaw! So delicious. For desserts the cupcakes were all wonderful. I also had a special order “naked cake” made of a sugar free Italian cream cake and it was a hit with everyone.

Thank you for providing a great service!

It exceeded ALL of our expectations!
